BLACK CLOVER

Black Clover follows Asta and Yuno who were abandoned in the same church on the same day. Created together as children, they met the "king of the wizard" - a title given to the strongest wizard of the kingdom - and promised to compete with each other by the position of the next king of the wizard

Yuno

Yuno is a talented wizard with a strong sense of justice. He is Asta's rival and childhood friend, and he possesses a four-leaf clover grimoire, which is a rare and powerful artifact.
